Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich is one of the most delicous items you can install in your head cavity. For the rest of this talk, I will be refering to the peanut and butter jelly sandwich to pbj. To save time.

The combination of flavors, from the sweet touch of jelly, to the earthy and nutty peanut butter, down to the fluffiest piece of bread known to man, you have discovered greatness!


Ingrediants
  • Bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly
Steps
  1. Spread jam onto one slice of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter onto other slice of bread
  3. Jam those two together
  4. Eat pbj
