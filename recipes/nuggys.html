Nuggys

Description

If you are looking for simple and good, look no further. Nuggys is everything that the lazy person has been waiting for!

The combination of flavors, from the crunchy salted breading, to the soft and and tasty inside tenderness, you have discovered greatness!


Ingrediants
  • Bag of Gardein Nuggys
  • Time
Steps
  1. Insert nuggys into oven and set temperature to 450 degrees
  2. Set timer for 21 minutes exactly and go smoke a bowl
  3. Timer goes off and enjoy greatness
